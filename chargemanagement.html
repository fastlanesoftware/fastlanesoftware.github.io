Charge Management

Split Services

Share the monthly costs of a service over multiple cost centres, no restriction to the number of splits per service.


Assign Costs

Implement programmable rules in how to deal with specific products, charges or discounts, can be monthly or as an one off.


Allocate Purchases

Manage how hardware, license and project charges are allocated or apportioned. Charges assigned at account level can be converted into individual orders for appropriate financial allocation if reference identification is visible.
