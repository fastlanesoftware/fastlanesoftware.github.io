Monthly Reporting

Billview is a monthly recurring, comprehensive online telecommunication asset management and bill reporting service that enables large organisations to manage all aspects of their telecommunications usage and expenditure - easily, efficiently and effectively.

BillView telecommunication reporting and expense management system provides multi-carrier integrated billing information in a single source web based facility. Carrier bills are effectively recompiled into a personalised, interactive and customer specific format.

All existing carriers can be incorporated into our reporting systems, including Optus, Aapt, Amcom, Vodfone, Pivotel, Three, GlobalStar, Nexium and others. Telstra being the dominant Australian carrier however, attracts particular emphasis.

BillView combines complex information processing with web based presentation to deliver an intuitive, flexible and accessible tool for inventory management, analysis and reporting of telecommunication services.

How it Works

The 4 Major Sections

Inventory Management

  1. Record your organisations services within a 3 or 4 tier hierarchy.

  2. Create, edit, rearrange the costcentre and service information easily through drag and drop mouse clicks.

  3. Intuitive and flexible filtering and search capability.

  4. Recorded tracking of chnages for simple oversight and roll back capability.

User Management

  1. Create and manage unlimited number of user access and privilege rights to your organisations custom BillView website.

  2. Multi tier access levels from 'Administrator' to 'Costcentre Manager' to singer service 'User'.

  3. Control privileges such as creating, editing and viewing for each persons given permission to log in. They can have full editing control, partial control or viewing access only.

Reporting

  1. Your carrier bills are collected electronically and consolidated into a browsable online report.

  2. Reports are filtered by the access level of each user. Administrators can see all reports, Tier Managers can only see their own costcentre, Users can only see their own services.

  3. Exception reports allow expensive calls, long held calls, unused services or other anomalous patterns to be identified.

Email Distribution

  1. Emails can be distributed containing personalised 'links' which open BillView automatically for each individual user.

  2. Managers are emailed summary reports on their cost centre and contained services each month.

  3. Users receive fully itemised reports for their own services each month.

Benefits of Billview

Processing

  1. Allows for the amalgamation of telecommunication accounts, instead of tens or hundreds of accounts you manage a handful;

  2. Enables the streamlining and automation of the account payment process, making staff more efficient;

  3. Online reporting allows management by exception;

Accountability

  1. Structured heirachy and online reporting allows overview of telecommunication costs and inventory from the whole organisation to each individual user;

  2. Inventory and telecommunication costs are effectively audited each month;

  3. End users and cost centre managers are made aware of their costs via online reporting and emailed reports directly to their inbox;

Manageability

  1. Visibility, providing consolidated summaries for monitoring and planning purposes;

  2. 12 month trend graphs across a wide variety of cost points;

  3. Targeted reporting allows management by exception;

  4. Enabling redirection of staff productivity from time consuming operational bill handling and checking to strategic planning;

Optimisation

  1. Reduces the expense of external consultants for auditing and review;

  2. Providing alerts for inefficiently configured services and equipment;

  3. Providing accurate benchmarks;
